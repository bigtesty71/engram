Key Model Name Patterns
Stable versions typically do not have a suffix, such as gemini-2.5-pro.
Versioned or preview models often have dates or version numbers, like gemini-2.5-flash-preview-09-2025.
When calling the API, use models/ (e.g., models/gemini-2.0-flash).
