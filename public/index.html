Memory Persistence

Why E.N.G.R.A.M.?

Traditional AI forgets everything between sessions. E.N.G.R.A.M. doesn't.

🕸️

Graph-Native Memory

Memories are stored as typed, interconnected nodes — not flat text. People, events, preferences, and concepts form a living knowledge graph.

🎯

Confidence Scoring

Every memory has a trust score. Repeated mentions increase confidence. Contradictions lower it. E.N.G.R.A.M. prefers omission over incorrect recall.

🔍

Hybrid Retrieval

Combines semantic similarity with structural graph traversal. Finds not just what was said — but the context, relationships, and patterns around it.

⏰

Temporal Awareness

Memories have timestamps and validity windows. E.N.G.R.A.M. knows when things happened, when they expired, and how preferences drift over time.

💾

Automatic Consolidation

Session conversations are periodically summarized and merged into the persistent graph. Nothing important is lost — even across reboots.

🔒

Governed & Secure

Node-level access controls, role-based filtering, and sensitive data encryption. Your memories are private and protected.
